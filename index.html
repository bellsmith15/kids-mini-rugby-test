About Us

Who are we?


We are a club that supports the young people in the Guildford area to play mini rugby. We give all ages a chance to play, starting from 5 years old to 10. Mini Rugby is played on Saturdays and Sundays.



















What do we do?


We offer a friendly safe environment for young players to develop their rugby and life skills. Develop childrens communicaion, leadership and teamworking skills. Improve ball-handling and kicking skills Meet and make new friends in a family environment.



















The Coaching Team


Mini Rugby is coached by the club players who give their time to provide rugby sessions for young children. Making sure that the values that they play by are passed down to the youngest in the club. During the game, players will learn how to make tricky decisions which will in turn help them to gain confidence as they learn the rules of the game. Through rugby, children can develop the capability to analyse and deduce general principles in life which can in turn boost their mental, social and physical agility.



























What we offer:


  • Weekly sessions during the rugby season
  • Coached by players at the club who volunteer each weekend
  • Trial sessions before you commit
  • Equipment provided at the club grounds
  • Clubhouse cafe serves hot food and refreshments
